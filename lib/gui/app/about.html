About Etcher-ng

Etcher-ng is a fork of balenaEtcher, an OS image writing app. I have updated it to Electron , updated the name and icon, added more menu commands, and updated npm packages including the etcher-sdk. I also restored settings that were removed, tooltips for buttons, and Windows 7/8/8.1 support!
